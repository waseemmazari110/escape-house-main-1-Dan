## â“ FAQs

### "Why can't I submit a listing?"
**Answer:** You need an active paid subscription. Go to `/pricing` to choose a plan.

### "I paid but still can't submit"
**Answer:** Payment may still be processing. Wait a few minutes and refresh. If issue persists, contact support.

### "How long until my listing is approved?"
**Answer:** Admins review within 24-48 hours during business days.

### "My listing was rejected, what now?"
**Answer:** Check the rejection reason in your dashboard. Make the requested changes and resubmit.

### "Can I edit my approved listing?"
**Answer:** Yes! But major changes (title, pricing, capacity) may require re-approval.

### "Can visitors book my property?"
**Answer:** No. The system is view-only. Visitors contact you directly for bookings.
